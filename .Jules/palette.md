## 2024-05-18 - Missing ARIA Labels on Icon-only Links\n**Learning:** Icon-only links and buttons in the Playground UI (like the Home logo, Settings gear, Share as gist, and Download file) lack `aria-label` attributes for screen readers, and their child SVGs/images lack `aria-hidden="true"` or `alt=""`, making them inaccessible to assistive technologies. Visual tooltips (like Materialize CSSs `data-tooltip`) do not provide accessible names.\n**Action:** Add `aria-label` to the parent anchor tags to provide descriptive names, and add `aria-hidden="true"` or `alt=""` to purely decorative child images/SVGs to hide them from screen readers.
